There are many opportunities and benefits that cybersecurity provides, some of which are commonplace and others that are more overlooked.

1. Safe, secure and almost careless browsing as well as transactions while using the internet.

2. Protection against data theft.

3. Protection against malware (viruses, worms, trojans, and spyware/other unwanted programs.

4. Even provides accessibility (ease of access) with prevention of crashing and freezing.

5. Undoubtedly the most crucial and overlooked opportunity/benefit that cybersecurity provides is the anonymity and privacy of the user.
